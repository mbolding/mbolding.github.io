Space Programming
Templates & Resources

Standardized templates for documenting space requirements, equipment needs, and adjacencies for your research center design.

01. Room Data Sheet (RDS)

A detailed record of the requirements for a specific room or space type. One sheet should be completed for each unique room type (e.g., Wet Lab, Microscopy Suite, Break Room).

02. Adjacency Matrix

A tool to track the required proximity between different spaces. Use this to diagram relationships before drawing floor plans.

| Space Type | Wet Lab | Micro Suite | Office | Break Room |
|------------|---------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Wet Lab | - | Direct | Close | Neutral |
| Micro Suite| Direct | - | Neutral | Avoid |
| Office | Close | Neutral | - | Close |
| Break Room | Neutral | Avoid | Close | - |

Legend:
- Direct: Must be immediately adjacent (door-to-door).
- Close: Short walking distance (same hallway).
- Neutral: No specific requirement.
- Avoid: Should be separated (noise/vibration/contamination).
