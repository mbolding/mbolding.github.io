UAB Neuroengineering PhD Program

The Only Standalone Neuroengineering PhD in the US

The Ph.D. in Neuroengineering is a joint program between the UAB School of Engineering and Heersink School of Medicine. This program integrates advanced topics in neurophysiology, neural dynamics, neuropathology, systems engineering, computational analysis, advanced imaging, and data analysis to train the next generation of scientists tackling the brain's greatest challenges.
